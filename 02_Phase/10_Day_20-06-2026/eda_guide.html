📦 Outliers & Boxplot Explanation
Step 14
Hinglish Samjho: "Boxplot standard visual mapping indicators framework. Isse outlier values aur metrics clean limits trace ho jati hain."
Boxplot Breakdown: Box coordinates cover the Interquartile Range (IQR = Q3 - Q1). The green line represents Median. Whiskers map Lower & Upper thresholds. Points outside these boundaries (marked in red) are Outliers that distort calculations.
# IQR Outlier Detection Q1 = df["Age"].quantile(0.25) Q3 = df["Age"].quantile(0.75) IQR = Q3 - Q1 lower_limit = Q1 - (1.5 * IQR) upper_limit = Q3 + (1.5 * IQR) print(f"IQR: {IQR}, Limits: {lower_limit} to {upper_limit}") # Remove records outside limits df_cleaned = df[(df["Age"] >= lower_limit) & (df["Age"] <= upper_limit)]
Text Output
IQR: 18.0, Limits: -6.0 to 66.0 Original Rows: 418 Cleaned Rows: 416 (2 Outliers Removed)
